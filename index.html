Things to Know

How long does the fudge last for?

We recommend storing your fudge in the fridge for up to 4 weeks. Keep extras in the freezer to enjoy for up to 6 months, as fresh and tasty as day 1. Leaving your fudge at room temperature for up to 4 weeks is totally safe, but we would advise storing it in the firdge.

Do you accept custom orders for large events?

Yes! We often cater to larger events with custom requests for shapes, colors and sizing. Get in touch with us and we will provide you with a quote and further information as soon as we can.

What are the nutrition facts?

One piece contains about 180 calories, 7g of fat, 28.5g of carbohydrates and 24.6g of sugar

Is it organic, kosher, or anything else?

We use only organic ingredients to ensure that each piece as as healthy as can be! The fudge is indeed made with only kosher ingredients and with kosher pots and utensils. There is not a formal certification at this time.
